If you are thinking about joining Stafford Singers here are some things you might like to know.

Other useful stuff ....

For concerts you will need a black folder. You can buy one from us or provide your own.
Ladies wear black trousers, top and shoes with a green jacket which we can supply.
Gentlemen wear black trousers and shoes with a white shirt and green tie which we can supply.
